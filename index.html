Dr. Kumar Balepur

Software Professional Since 1990!

Started working on the main frame computers during my Ph. D. time (1984-1990) and got addicted to software. As and when the new systems and technologies emerged, started adopting and using it in my personal as well as professional life. I am fortunate enough to have seen and worked with a host of computer hardware (Mainframes, Super Computers, Super Minis, Desktops, Laptops), Operating Systems (Tops 10, Unix, Linux, DOS, Mac OS and OSX, Windows).It would be superfluous for me to mention that I am still a hands-on person who work on a host of technologies such as Java, Java EE, MEAN Stack, Android and iOS, Design and Architectural Patterns, TOGAF, Cyber Security etc.
